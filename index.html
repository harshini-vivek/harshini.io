Publications

Strategies to enhance Web-based Learning

This paper examines the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on the education system worldwide and emphasizes the importance of web-based learning. It compares three modes of web-based learning: Synchronous, Asynchronous, and Blended. The study involves a questionnaire survey with 500 students and faculties from schools and colleges in Madurai. The results indicate that the Blended mode, combining interactive and self-paced learning, is highly preferred due to its flexibility and availability of online materials. The paper suggests integrating edutainment and implementing enhancements to maximize the benefits of web-based learning.


Publisher:   Research and Reflections on Education (UGC Care Approved) · Paper completed for an International Conference on “Emerging Trends in Online Teaching Assessment and Learning” (eTOTAL)

Publishing Date:   March 2023
